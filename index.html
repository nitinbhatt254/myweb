The Hungriest Beast 🐺

In a forgotten alley where even WiFi fears to connect… lived the Hungriest Beast — Umesh.

Cats avoided him. Dogs respected him. Not because he was strong… but because his definition of “food” was… questionable.

One night, a cat dropped something. A dog sniffed it… and walked away.

Umesh didn’t.

He stared. He smiled.

And whispered… “Waste not.” 😈

Since then, the alley has been silent. No cats. No dogs.

Only footsteps… behind you. 👣
